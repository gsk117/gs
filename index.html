About Me

Research Scholar | IIT Jodhpur & AIIMS Jodhpur

He is a MedTech enthusiast, passionate about product design and development. He is self-motivated, dedicated, a problem solver and a proactive person. He is currently pursuing PhD in Medical Technologies (Interdisciplinary joint research program) jointly conducted by IIT Jodhpur & AIIMS Jodhpur. He is part of the Visual Interaction & Understanding (VIU) Lab at IIT Jodhpur, the Cardiology Department at AIIMS Jodhpur, and Radiodiagnosis (Diagnostic and Interventional Radiology) at AIIMS Jodhpur as a Research Scholar. He is jointly supervised by Dr Deepak Mishra , Dr Rahul Choudhary, and Dr Pushpinder Singh Khera.

His research interests are Medical Imaging, Machine Learning, Medical Robotics, Therapeutic and Diagnostic Ultrasound, and Bioheat transfer. He has sound knowledge of analysis and simulation software like COMSOL Multiphysics, Ansys, OpenFOAM & Paraview, meshing software Gmsh, Modelling and drafting software SolidWorks, AutoCAD, Creo and CATIA.

Projects undertaken

Postgraduate Project (Thesis)

Study of Pulsed Ultrasound assisted Thermo-therapy for Subsurface Tumor Ablation

Supervisor: Dr Anup Paul, Assistant Professor & Head, Department of Mechanical Engineering
Project Brief: Numerical simulation performed in COMSOL to predict the effect of Pulsed Ultrasound on the tissue ablation. Consider five types of tissue model, two types of tumor size variation, pulse width ranging from 0.6 to 0.9 with transducer power of 10W & 15W. Helmholtz Equation, Pennes Equation, and CEM thermal dose function were solved. Experiments were conducted in the MUSE lab, IIT Gandhinagar under the guidance of Prof. Himanshu Shekhar.
